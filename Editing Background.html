Steps on how to remove a background using Picsart on your andriod.

1.Open the app and tap the plus sign at the bottom to upload your photo.

2.Select the photo that you want to put a background.

3.Click on the "Fit" icon to proceed.

4.Click the image icon to pick a background you want.

5.But if you want to pick a background from your galery tap on the icon on the lower left of your screen pointing the red arrow.

6.Tap on the arrow at the upper right of your screen to proceed.

7.Tap on the "Galery" to save it.

8.Click "Done" to finish the process.
