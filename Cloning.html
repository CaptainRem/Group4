Steps on how to remove a background using Picsart on your andriod.

1.Open the app and tap the plus sign at the bottom to upload your photo.

2.Tap on the Tools icon to proceed.

3.Tap on the cloning icon to proceed.

4.Use this "Round Shaped" icon to select which part of the picture you want to clone.

5.Use the "Pen" icon to draw where you want to put the clone haha.

6.Use eraser if you mistake cloning haha.

7.After tapping on the "Pen" icon you can now swipe beside the photo you want to clone.

8.Tap on the upper right arrow to proceed.
